Original Soundtracks

Sky Original Game Soundtrack Vol. 1 is the game's first album release, composed by award-winning composer Vincent Diamante and brought to life by FAME'S Macedonian Symphonic Orchestra.

Sky Original Game Soundtrack Vol. 2 features a 26-track album that continues the game’s atmospheric scoring of the main story arc, composed by award-winning composer Vincent Diamante.

Sky Original Game Soundtrack Vol. 3 features a 36-track album encompassing favorites of the past adventure seasons in the game, composed by award-winning composer Vincent Diamante.
